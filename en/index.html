FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does SwimAnalytics get my swimming data?

SwimAnalytics syncs with Apple Health to import swimming workouts recorded by any compatible device or app. This includes smart watches, fitness trackers, and manual entries. The app processes this data locally to calculate advanced metrics.

What is the CSS test and how do I perform it?

CSS (Critical Swim Speed) is a scientific protocol using 2 maximum effort swims: 400m and 200m with 10-20 minutes rest between. The app calculates your aerobic threshold from these times and automatically adjusts all training zones. Repeat every 6-8 weeks to track progress.

Is my data uploaded to the cloud?

No. SwimAnalytics processes all data locally on your iPhone. There are no external servers, no cloud accounts, no data transfers. You control exports: generate JSON, CSV, HTML, or PDF files and share them however you want.

Can I use SwimAnalytics for open water swimming?

Yes. SwimAnalytics works with any swimming workout in Apple Health, including open water. The app adapts to available metrics whether you're in a pool or open water, providing relevant analysis for each environment.
